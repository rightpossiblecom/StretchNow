
# StretchNow – Simple App Plan https://stretch-now.vercel.app/privacy-policy

## 1️⃣ App Goal

StretchNow reminds users to take short stretch breaks throughout the day.

It suggests simple stretch prompts at set intervals and allows users to mark them as completed.

The purpose is:

* Encourage movement
* Break long sitting periods
* Build a light daily routine

No coaching.
No medical claims.
No fitness tracking.

---

## 2️⃣ What The App Does

* Sends periodic stretch reminders (e.g., every 2–3 hours)
* Shows a short stretch suggestion
* User taps “Done”
* Tracks daily streak of completed stretch sessions

All stored locally.

---

## 3️⃣ What The App Does NOT Do

* No physiotherapy advice
* No injury treatment claims
* No posture correction promises
* No health diagnostics
* No calorie tracking
* No integration with wearables
* No AI-generated routines

It is a reminder app, not a medical or fitness app.

---

# 4️⃣ Core User Story

> As a user, I receive a gentle reminder to stretch, perform a short stretch, mark it complete, and continue my day.

Simple loop.

---

# 5️⃣ App Structure

## 🏠 1. Home Screen

Top:

* Today’s date
* Daily progress (e.g., 2 of 4 stretches done)

Center:

* Current suggested stretch
  Example:

  * “Neck roll”
  * “Shoulder stretch”
  * “Stand and reach”

Below:

* ✅ “Mark as Done”
* ⏭ “Skip”

Bottom:

* Current streak 🔥

---

## ⏱ 2. Schedule Page

Simple controls:

* Choose reminder frequency:

  * Every 2 hours
  * Every 3 hours
  * Custom interval
* Set active hours:

  * Start time
  * End time

Minimal but useful.

---

## 📅 3. History Page

Simple list:

* Date – 4/4 completed
* Date – 2/4 completed

At top:

* Current streak
* Longest streak

No analytics dashboard.

---

## ⚙️ 4. Settings Page

* Enable/disable notifications
* Vibration toggle
* Reset streak
* Privacy Policy
* App version

Clean and complete.

# 6️⃣ One Nice-to-Have Feature

### 🧘 “Quick 60-Second Mode”

If user doesn’t have time:

Add a button:

> “1-Minute Stretch”

It shows one ultra-simple stretch instruction instantly without waiting for scheduled reminder.

This makes the app feel proactive without adding complexity.
