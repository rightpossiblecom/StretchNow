# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026

Welcome to StretchNow! This Privacy Policy explains how StretchNow ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## 1. Information We Collect

StretchNow is designed to be simple and respect your privacy. We intentionally collect as little data as possible.

- **Usage Data**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to understand how our App is used. This data does not personally identify you.
- **Local Data**: The App may store settings (like reminder times, notification preferences) locally on your device.

## 2. How We Use Information

The information mentioned above is used solely for:
- Providing and maintaining the App functionality.
- Customizing your experience within the App.

## 3. Third-Party Services

StretchNow does not share your personal info with third parties. However, we may use standard analytics or error reporting services which collect device type, OS version, and crash data to help us improve the app.

## 4. Security

We value your trust in providing us with your data, so we strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@stretchnow.app.
